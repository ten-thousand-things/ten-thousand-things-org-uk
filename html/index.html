forging free and open source software in collaboration with blockades, dyne think [&do] tank and protozoa

ten thousand things applies a dialectical methodology combining social research and networked collaboration with software development

we work with communities directly to build software that expands the commons

we produce tools that emphasise interdependency alongside individual autonomy

we host workshops on: data privacy, password and private key management, online identity protection, social secret sharing, and the use of distributed p2p technologies
